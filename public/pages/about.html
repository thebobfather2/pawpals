About us!

The purpose of a Paw Pals is to connect loving pet owners with animals in need of a home. The website serves as a platform for animal shelters and rescue organizations to showcase their adoptable pets and find them new families.

Under Pal Place you can find features photos and descriptions of each pet, as well as information on their personality, habits, and needs. Prospective adopters can browse the available animals and learn more about them, and if they find a pet they're interested in, they can contact the shelter or rescue organization to learn more and begin the adoption process.

Our ultimate goal is to find forever homes for as many animals as possible, helping to reduce pet homelessness and improve the lives of both pets and their new owners.
